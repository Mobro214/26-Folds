About Us

Advancing Technology, Enriching Businesses, and Empowering Communities.

At 26 Folds, we are a dynamic and innovative engineering firm committed to developing cutting-edge solutions for a diverse range of industries, including Healthcare, Education, Financial Technology, Environment, and Society. Our passion lies in harnessing the power of technology and data to drive positive impact and transformative change.

Our team comprises highly skilled professionals from various backgrounds, such as engineering, data science, medicine, and business, bringing a unique blend of expertise to every project we take on. With our multidisciplinary approach, we thrive in tackling complex challenges and creating comprehensive solutions that cater to the evolving needs of our clients and industries.

While we prioritize healthcare and its outcomes, our projects encompass a wide spectrum of domains, from AI-powered disease detection models to intuitive financial applications. We believe in the potential to revolutionize healthcare delivery and other sectors through our innovative ventures.

In addition to our healthcare-focused projects, we offer a comprehensive suite of services to meet our clients' diverse needs. Our expertise includes web design and development, business development consulting, data analytics, and engineering/product development, among others. By combining technical proficiency with strategic insights, we guide our clients through challenges, capitalize on opportunities, and help them achieve their goals.

We strongly believe in the power of collaboration to drive meaningful change. We actively seek partnerships with healthcare professionals, researchers, industry experts, and technology enthusiasts to foster innovation and create a collective impact. Working together, we aim to bring forth groundbreaking solutions that transform healthcare delivery, enhance patient outcomes, and promote sustainable progress.

At 26 Folds, we are united by a shared vision of a future where technology unlocks new possibilities. Our passion lies in making a difference and empowering engineers, healthcare professionals, businesses, and society through our innovative projects and services. We are dedicated to shaping a brighter future and contributing to positive change in multiple domains, driven by our commitment to innovation and positive impact.
